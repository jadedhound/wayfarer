Character Creation Guide

  • Download and print the character sheet. The sheet is A5 in size, print 2 on A4 paper.
  • Choose a character from the classes.
  • Fill in traits.
  • Weapon and armour proficiencies are found in basics. Archetype proficiencies are found under specfic archetypes.
  • Roll 2d6 + 3, go here if you have no dice (might not work on mobile). Do this 6 times. Put the highest in the class specific one: STR for warriors, DEX for scoundrels, WIS for zealots and INT for mages. Put the second highest in CON. Distribute the rest as you see fit.
  • Your speed is 30 ft. and your AC is dependent on the armour your wearing. Gambeson: 1, chainmail: 2. If you are holding a shield increase your AC by 1. E.g. chainmail + shield = 3 AC. HP is in class basics.
  • Have a look at the advancement table. At 1st level you get everything in that row: starting HP, equipment and a special skill. Note the skill down in your notes (or somewhere for reference).
    If you are a spellcaster (zealot or mage). Choose 2 spells from your respective schools (divine or arcane). Have a read of the spellcasting stipulations as well.
  • You can carry CON amount of items. The first four of these are quick access items, which means you can take them in and out of your inventory very quickly. The rest of your items is in you backpack.
    Copy the equipment list, do note that 2-handed weapons and chainmail specifically take up 2 item slots instead of just 1. Roll on the sundries table (see link below) the number of times indicated in the equipment list.
    Every class starts with a: Primitive Lantern. Note this is the last slot labelled lantern.
  • You start with 5 GP.
