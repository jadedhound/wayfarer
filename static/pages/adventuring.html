Load

Each item the size of a human head or 5 lbs is considered 1 load. You are able to carry [ CON ] worth of load. You cannot carry more than your carry load, i.e. being encumbered.

Magic Items

Powerful magic items require attunement to use, you can attune to a maximum 3 of such items. Attunement can only be done during a rest.
