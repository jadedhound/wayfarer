Armour

Armour. Each point of armour mitigates 1 point of damage. An AC of 3 means you take 3 less damage per hit.
Sundering. You can destroy armour to reduce incoming damage at a rate of [ AC x 5 ].

Healing and HP

HP. Hit Protection, a measure of how good your character is at avoiding damage. Damage taken is first soaked by HP before it rolls over to damage/reduce your CON. You regain all HP after a rest.
CON. Damage to your CON results in noticeable damage. Any time you take damage to your CON, you must make a CON check to see if you survive the blow. You regain 1 CON per rest.
