Spell Stipulations

Spells sometimes have requirements or limitations.
Resist. The creature rolls the relevant defence check, failure means they succumb to the spell.
Quick. The spell can be cast as a quick action.
Concentration. You can only concentrate on one spell at a time. Taking damage or trying to do another distracting task (such as making a skill check) disrupts the spell.
